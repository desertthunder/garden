From a [BlueSky post](https://aturi.to/did:plc:gq4fo3u6tqzzdkjlwzpb23tj/app.bsky.feed.post/3miw4qpochk2v) by [dame.is](https://dame.is/)

> i want to know where the software was made
>
> i want to know who made the software
>
> i want to know why the software was made
>
> i want to know why software was chosen as the means to the end
>
> i want to know what will happen when the software is no longer available or is changed beyond recognition

I think this is a good framework for thinking through and explaining the context, purpose, and impact of software in a way that is transparent and informative for users.
